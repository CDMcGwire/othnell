Mundane Traits are acquired every level. They help define your character and improve their capabilities, but not in ways that would be particularly exceptional.

Put another way, a Mundane Trait would differentiate a body builder from a runner, but would not do well to tell you which body builder could carry the heaviest load or which could bend steel with his legs.
