A Clash Roll is when two opposing creatures roll off to determine who succeeds and who loses and by how much. The size and number of dice used by each creature is determined by their Brawn and any relevant equipment or traits.

Minimum Base Die is d2 for -1 Brawn. Size increases by 1 for every 1 point higher Brawn.

So, d2 at -1, d4 at 0, d6 at +1, etc...
